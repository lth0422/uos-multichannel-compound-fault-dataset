## Prohibited or premature claims

- UOS v2 is superior to all existing datasets.
- Multi-channel input is always better than single-channel input.
- Multi-sensor, multi-modal, multi-channel, multi-position, and multi-direction are interchangeable.
- Compound faults or multi-position vibration are globally new.
- A final UOS sampling rate, RPM grid, record length, sensor layout, or contribution has already been established.
